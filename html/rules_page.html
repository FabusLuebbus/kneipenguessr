How to Play Kneipenguessr

The goal is simple: guess which bar is closer to your location!

1. The game will use your device's geolocation feature to determine your current position.

2. Two bars will be displayed on the screen. Your task is to pick the one closer to you.

3. If you choose correctly, the closer bar stays, and a new one is introduced. Keep going and test your bar proximity skills!

4. If you guess wrong, the game ends, and your score is displayed.
